Port Huron Beach Home - Luxurious vacation house located on lakefront
with its own private beach. Located about 90 minutes or 80 miles from Detroit Metro Airport. There are 7 bedrooms as follows: 6 with queen beds, 1 with two double beds, 3 full bathrooms, fully equipped kitchen with two dishwashers and two ovens. The family room has a HDTV, Blue-Ray DVD. Two bedrooms also have a 35-inch tube TV. Wifi is available. Propane and charcoal barbecues are available.

Great for family vacations! However, the community does not allow weddings or receptions. This house has been in my family for over 50 years.

Fully furnished luxurious house, large kitchen and breakfast area, separate dining room, Florida room featuring indoor and outdoor furniture and 180 degree view of the lake. There is a driveway with parking for 7 cars and there is room in the garage for one car.

A sidewalk on the lakeside goes north from the house for about a half mile and is great for walking or kids biking. The sand beach is private.

Best weather around! Beach activities, golf, fishing, and public tennis courts nearby. There are plenty of stores to shop at during your stay. Bring your passports the Blue Water Bridge to Canada is only about 2 miles south.

Summer rates range from $3,600 to $4,200 per week. Summer rental months are June, July and August for a full week or longer. Fall, Winter & Spring rates range from $1,250 to $1,800 for a full week. There are no daily or weekend rentals.
Sleeps 14 in summer, late spring and early fall. Sleeps only 10 in winter, since there is no heat in three of the second floor bedrooms.

Great Lake Freighters pass by the house during most days; some of them are over 1000 feet long.
